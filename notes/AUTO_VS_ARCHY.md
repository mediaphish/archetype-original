# Auto vs Archy (internal note)

- **Archy** — What visitors meet on the public site: conversation, FAQs, routing, and help grounded in Bart’s published library. Paid-tier visitors (session list or future billing) get deeper retrieval and rolling thread memory.

- **Auto** — Internal only (AO Automation dashboard): research-style help for Bart and operators—planning, packaging, CORPUS TL;DR briefings, drafts queue, and secured publish. It is not a second public product name.

Copy and prompts should keep that boundary clear so the team does not blur internal tooling with the visitor-facing assistant.
