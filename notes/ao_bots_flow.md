## AO Automation — bot roles and handoffs

This document is the single place to describe how the bots/agents interact.
When you send new notes for each bot, we will update this file and then map the notes into the live Scout → Analyst → Studio → Publisher behavior.

### Scout (Reporter)
- **Job**: Find leadership leads conservatively, follow promising links, and deliver decision-ready items into Analyst.
- **Hard rules**: Conservative, no paywalls, no politics/rage bait, always include a working link.
- **Output into Analyst**: Source link + clean excerpt + pull quote + “why it matters” + risks + recommended next step.

### Analyst (Decision desk)
- **Job**: Decide what happens next: Approve → Studio, Approve → Publisher, Hold, or Reject.
- **Standard**: Approval should feel realistic; “what’s good / what’s weak / next step” should make the decision easy.

### Studio (Finish the work)
- **Job**: Turn approved items into ready-to-post drafts (and assets like quote cards when appropriate).

### Publisher (Schedule + distribute)
- **Job**: Schedule posts across channels and publish with the right timing.

### Ideas (Your own seeds)
- **Job**: Accept your own ideas (concepts or complete drafts), shape them, and route them into the pipeline.

### Settings
- **Job**: Defaults, safety, and account-level knobs.
