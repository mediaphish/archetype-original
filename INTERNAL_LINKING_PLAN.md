## üéØ SEO Best Practices for Internal Linking

1. **Use Descriptive Anchor Text:**
   - Avoid generic "click here" or "read more"
   - Use keywords naturally: "servant leadership research" not "this article"

2. **Link Contextually:**
   - Place links where they add value, not just at the end
   - Link when concepts are first introduced

3. **Maintain Link Balance:**
   - Don't over-link (2-3 per section is good)
   - Prioritize high-value connections

4. **Use Related Content:**
   - Journal posts already have `related` field in frontmatter
   - Leverage this for "Related Reading" sections

5. **Track Link Performance:**
   - Monitor which links get clicked
   - Adjust based on user behavior
