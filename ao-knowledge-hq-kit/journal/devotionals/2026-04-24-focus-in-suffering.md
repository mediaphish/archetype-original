## Scripture

**John 19:25–27**

## Reflection

Even in suffering, Jesus remains attentive to others.

He recognizes the need in front of Him and responds with care. The moment is not convenient. It is not easy. Yet it is clear.

Servant leadership does not pause care when circumstances become difficult.

Leaders often narrow their focus when pressure increases. Attention shifts inward. Responsibility becomes reduced to survival.

Jesus maintains awareness beyond Himself.

This is not about doing more. It is about remaining attentive to what matters, even in hard moments.

## Practical Application

- Look for a need in front of you that requires attention.
- Respond with care even if the moment is difficult.
- Stay outwardly aware when pressure increases.

## Takeaways

- Servant leadership remains attentive under pressure.
- Care does not stop when circumstances become difficult.

## Closing Thought

Leadership continues to see others, even in the hardest moments.
