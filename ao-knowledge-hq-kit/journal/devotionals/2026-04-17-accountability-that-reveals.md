## Scripture

**Matthew 25:31–33**

---

## Reflection

Jesus brings His teaching to a point of final clarity.

Leadership is not only about responsibility in the present. It will be evaluated.

This passage shifts from preparation to accountability.

Servant leadership recognizes that actions, decisions, and priorities will be revealed for what they are.

There is no confusion in this moment. Separation is clear. What has been lived out becomes visible.

Leaders often operate within systems that blur outcomes. Jesus removes that ambiguity.

This is not intended to create fear. It is intended to bring alignment.

Leadership becomes clearer when accountability is understood.

---

## Practical Application

- Reflect on how your current decisions would be evaluated.
- Align your actions with what you know is right.
- Remove anything that relies on being hidden.

---

## Takeaways

- Leadership will be revealed and evaluated.
- Clarity about accountability brings alignment.

---

## Closing Thought

What is lived out in leadership will eventually be made clear.
