What I found when I went looking wasn't new. The decisions I'd made to put people first weren't original. Just rare. The Room, built on something that shook me — no leader ever has a fully honest room. And The Leadership Gap, still unpublished, tied to the Archetype Leadership Index I'm building to measure the distance between how leaders see themselves and how their teams actually experience them.
