## Summary

This is Episode 0 of The Archetype Original Podcast — Bart's first recording, solo, unscripted, and intentionally raw.

He walks through thirty-three years of building in Missouri, the 2022 sale that shook his sense of identity, and what he found when he went looking: servant leadership is not soft, it is demanding, and the team built the company, not one person at the top.

He introduces the four books behind the Archetype Original corpus — Accidental CEO, Remaining Human, The Room, and the forthcoming Leadership Gap tied to ALI — and closes with the rule he believes most: if you grow people, you grow the organization. If you use people, you lose it.
