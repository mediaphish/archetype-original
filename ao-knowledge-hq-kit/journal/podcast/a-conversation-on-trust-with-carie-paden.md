## Summary

Bart sits down with Carie for an unscripted conversation about trust, the kind that gets talked about constantly in leadership circles but rarely defined.

They walk through what trust looked like in the early days of MWI, when there was no playbook and every decision carried real weight.

> Trust is not the absence of conflict. It is what lets conflict happen without anyone keeping score.

Carie offers a perspective Bart does not always have access to: what it looks like from the outside when someone is leading well, and what it looks like when they are not.

The conversation moves through specific moments from building the company, what almost broke trust, and what rebuilt it.
