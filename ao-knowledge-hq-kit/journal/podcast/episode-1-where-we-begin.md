## Summary

This is the opening episode of The Archetype Original Podcast, recorded solo.

Bart walks through why the show exists, what three decades of building companies actually taught him about people and pressure, and why clarity matters more than intensity when you are trying to lead something that lasts.

> Leadership is not performed in the big moment. It is practiced in the small ones people learn to predict.

The episode sets the tone for the series: honest, specific, and grounded in lived experience rather than theory for its own sake.
