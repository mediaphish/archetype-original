Accidental CEO is a leadership memoir grounded in real experience.

It's not built on theory or frameworks alone. It's a firsthand account of leading through pressure, growth, cultural strain, and the responsibility that shows up before you feel ready.

It also includes practical leadership principles that can be applied immediately.
