Bart Paden is the founder of Archetype Original.

He is a builder, operator, and servant leader with over three decades of real-world leadership experience across business, technology, and fitness.

Archetype Original exists to translate lived leadership experience into practical frameworks that help leaders build stable, human-centered cultures.
