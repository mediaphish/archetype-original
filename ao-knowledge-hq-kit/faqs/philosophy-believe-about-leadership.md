At its core, Archetype Original believes leadership is less about control and more about responsibility.

Most leadership models focus on authority, influence, or output. This work focuses on the environment leaders create—often unintentionally—and how that environment shapes behavior, trust, and long-term performance.

Leadership is not neutral. Every decision, reaction, and habit is teaching people what is safe, what is valued, and what is expected.
