Archetype Original is values-driven, not doctrine-driven.

You'll feel moral gravity in the work—responsibility, dignity, restraint, truth—but it's designed to be useful to leaders regardless of belief system.

The philosophy is grounded in lived leadership realities rather than religious instruction.
